# Information gain
**Overview**
Description:: Root node picker for decision trees.

## Note

Information gain is used within [[Decision Trees|decision trees]] to generate the root feature node. It measures the difference between the uncertainty (entropy) before and after a split. It's essentially asking: "did this split help us make better predictions?" The higher the information gain, the better the split.
